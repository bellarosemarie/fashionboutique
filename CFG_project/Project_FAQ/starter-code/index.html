Shipping Delays

Please note that shipment to particularly international countries can be delayed due to Covid-19. If you have not received your order within 1 month of placing your order please get in contact with our customer servive team: Contact Us

Shipping Costs

We offer a variety of different shipping methods such as Standard Delivery, Express Delivery, International Delivery. If you are within the EU we are charging a flat-fee of 10 pounds. To all other international destinations we are charging a flat-fee of 20 pounds. Free delivery for orders above 100 pounds internationally.

Returns

Our return policy allows for free returns within the first 14 days after delivery. Due to the pandemic the return period was extended to 28 days after.
